Product Purpose Popular Brands Needs to be compatable with...
Processor (CPU) The processor is what drives your computer. Intel, AMD Socket must be compatable with motherboard.
Motherboard Holds all of the parts together. If the processor is the heart of the computer, this is the brain. ASUS, ASRock, Gigabyte The CPU Socket must match the CPU, RAM must be supported.
RAM The RAM is the short term memory, what enables it to function. Corsair, Gell, Kingston, G. Skill The RAM speed must be compatible with the motherboard.
Graphics Card (GPU) The graphics card handles all output to the monitor, including display and rendering. Important for gaming. Nvidia, AMD Must fit into motherboard and have correct output to monitor.
Power Supply (PSU) The power supply gives power to all of the components. Corsair, Sesonic, CoolerMaster, Antec, EVGA Make sure that it will provide enough power to your system.

Hard Disk Drive (HDD) or Solid State Drive (SSD) The Hard Drive or Solid State are what holds the information stored in the computer. This is where your operating system lives, along with everything you save to it. SeaGate, Intel, SanDisk Make sure that you have enough room to store everything you want to.
